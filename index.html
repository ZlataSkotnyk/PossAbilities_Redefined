My Achievements

I draw upon my own lived experiences in order to educate various audiences, including at the Board level. In addition, I have spoken on disabilities in various classroom settings and staff meetings within the John Cabot Secondary School community in Mississauga. I am the creator of the PossAbilities Catholic Leadership Conference (2011 & 2012) which was a Board-wide initiative for students in Dufferin-Peel with physical disability, chronic illness or disease. Through this conference, I supported students with disabilities in gaining the skills and accessing the resources and information necessary to support them in their lives/future. It empowered young people to use their voice and lived experience to make a difference in their school communities. I have also recently started my own business, PossAbilities Redefined, for my writing and speaking on issues related to disabilities.

About Me

I am a dedicated wife, mother, disability advocate, guest speaker and aspiring author. As a woman who lives with Spina Bifida, it is my dream to help redefine the way we think about disability. My goal is to help shift our focus from the limitations to the PossAbilities. I am a secondary school teacher with the Dufferin-Peel Catholic District School Board (DPCDSB) with over 15 years of teaching experience. Born with a physical disability (Spina Bifida), I have navigated the education system in Ontario as both a student and teacher.
